PESHAWARI KARAHI

Serving 4

Peshawari Chicken Karahi Recipe. How to make Chicken Karahi by Kitchen peshawari-chicken-karahi-recipe

Ingredients:

  • Chicken 1 Kg
  • Tomato 6 (Chopped)
  • Ghee/Oil 1 Cup
  • Ginger Garlic Paste 1 tbsp
  • Green Coriander 1 tbsp (Chopped)
  • Green Chilli 6 (Chopped)
  • Ginger 1 tsp (Chopped)
  • Black Pepper Powder 1 tsp
  • Dried Fenugreek Leaves 1 tbsp
  • Coriander Powder 1 tbsp
  • Garam Masala Powder 1/2 tsp
  • Red Chilli Powder 1 tsp
  • Salt 1 tsp

Preparation

  1. Take a pot and add ghee/oil and heat it and then add Chicken and fry it on high flame with stir for 5 minutes.
  2. After that add Ginger Garlic Paste, salt and stir it and cook for 2 minutes on medium flame.
  3. After that add Coriander Powder, Red Chilli Powder and stir it
  4. And then add Tomato and cover it with lid and cook for 10 minutes on medium flame.
  5. After that remove the lid and use high flame here and add Black Pepper Powder, Green Chillis and stir it for 1 minute on high flame and then add Dried Fenugreek Leaves,
